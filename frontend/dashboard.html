Tips & Resources

Tips for Reducing Your Carbon Footprint:
  • Use public transportation or carpool whenever possible.
  • Reduce energy consumption by switching off lights and unplugging devices.
  • Eat a more plant-based diet to lower greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Support renewable energy projects in your community.
